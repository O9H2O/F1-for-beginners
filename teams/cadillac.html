About Cadillac

Cadillac F1 Team is the newest and most eagerly anticipated entry in Formula 1 in years. Backed by General Motors — one of the largest car companies on Earth — Cadillac received FIA approval to join the championship as a fully independent constructor for the 2026 season.

The team is American-based and represents a major statement about Formula 1's growing popularity in the United States, where the sport has seen explosive growth since the Netflix Drive to Survive series began.

To lead the project, Cadillac secured two experienced veterans in Sergio Pérez — the Mexican fan favourite and former race winner — and Valtteri Bottas, five-time race winner and former Mercedes team-mate to Lewis Hamilton. Their combined experience gives the new team a crucial head start.
