About Ferrari

Ferrari is the most iconic name in Formula 1 — and the only team to have competed in every single season since the championship began in 1950. Founded by Enzo Ferrari, the Prancing Horse (Cavallino Rampante) is one of the most recognisable symbols in all of sport.

Their 16 Constructors' Championships make them the most successful constructor in F1 history. Champions like Michael Schumacher (5 titles), Niki Lauda, Kimi Räikkönen, and many others have driven the red car to glory at the pinnacle of the sport.

Ferrari's passionate Italian fanbase — the Tifosi — is legendary. When Ferrari races at Monza and Imola, the grandstands turn a sea of red. A Ferrari victory at the Italian Grand Prix produces scenes unlike anything else in motorsport.

In 2025, Lewis Hamilton made the most high-profile transfer in recent F1 history — leaving Mercedes (where he won 6 championships) to join Ferrari. The pairing with Leclerc is the most anticipated partnership in years.
