About Racing Bulls

Racing Bulls (formerly Toro Rosso, then AlphaTauri) is Red Bull's junior F1 team based in Faenza, Italy. The team's primary purpose is developing Red Bull junior drivers — giving them an F1 seat before (potentially) graduating to the senior team.

The team's record is remarkable: Sebastian Vettel drove here in 2007–2008, Max Verstappen was promoted mid-season in 2015. In 2020, Pierre Gasly won the Italian Grand Prix here — one of the most emotional victories in recent F1 memory.

In 2026, the Italian team fields Liam Lawson and teenage British-Swedish rookie Arvid Lindblad — continuing the tradition of giving exceptional young talent their first taste of the world's greatest racing series.
