About Williams

Williams is one of the most beloved and respected independent teams in Formula 1 history. Founded by Sir Frank Williams and Patrick Head in 1977, the team won seven Constructors' Championships — including five in seven years from 1992 to 1997.

Williams cars were driven by legends: Nigel Mansell, Alain Prost, Damon Hill, Jacques Villeneuve, and Ayrton Senna (in his final season). The FW14B of 1992 remains one of the most technically advanced cars ever built.

After years of financial struggle, Williams was sold to Dorilton Capital in 2020. Under James Vowles, Carlos Sainz arrived for 2025 and brought immediate results. The pairing of Sainz and Albon gives Williams their strongest lineup in decades.
